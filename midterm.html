Monthly Book Sale

Looking for books, records, videos, magazines, and more? Come to the Friends of the Palo Alto Library's monthly book sale!

What's at the book sale?

We sell hardcover and paperback books, magazines, CDs and DVDs, and a variety of other items. We also sell children's books, A/V materials, and toys!

Hours

Room Hours
Main Room 11 AM-4 PM Saturday and Sunday
Children's Room 10 AM-4 PM Saturday, 11 AM-4 PM Sunday
Bargain Room 10 AM-4 PM Saturday, 11 AM-4 PM Sunday

Attending a book sale

  • Come bright and early! We hand out up to 2 numbered tickets per person from 8-11 AM.
  • You'll be admitted in order of your ticket number.
  • A ticket will keep your place in line, if you decide to leave and come back.
  • No ticket? No problem. Simply join the back of the line.
  • For the health and safety of our patrons, we only allow 160 people into the sale at a time.
  • Please select no more than 12 books at a time! After noon, if there is no line, you may purchase more.

Directions

Our main room (MR) is where we feature our adult books and materials. The children's room (CR) houses children's items. Don't miss our bargain room (H2) for low prices on great reads!

Children's Items

We sell books, music, videos, and toys for preschool through elementary readers in our Children's Room! Our offerings include picture books, juvenile fiction and nonfiction, and non-English books, as well as parenting and teaching books.

Donations

Our donations come from library patrons, companies, and books weeded from the Palo Alto library. Interested in donating? We accept donations to the Main Room at the Friends book sale!

Why choose our book sale?

100% of the profits from our book sale go to your local libraries. We sell books at very low prices, with no sales tax, and our staff of volunteers are passionate about libraries and their local community!

Prices

  • Main room: Hardcover books start at $1; paperback books start at 50¢.
  • Children's room: Children's items start at 50¢ to $1.
  • Bargain books: All hardcover books are $1, and all paperbacks are 50¢. Children's books are 25¢.
  • Sales: Bargain room prices are half off on Sundays. Our best deal is the $5 special: for $5, you can buy a bag and fill it with books!
  • Gift cards: You can purchase gift certificates at the sale in $5 denominations.

We can't wait to see you there!
